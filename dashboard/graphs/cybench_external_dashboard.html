Cybench External

Cybench External is an external evaluation benchmark for assessing AI agents' cybersecurity capabilities, focusing on real-world penetration testing and defensive tasks. It includes a suite of challenging CTF-style problems, evaluated in unguided (autonomous exploration) and subtask-guided (step-by-step prompting) modes to measure planning, execution, and adaptability. Tasks span vulnerability discovery, exploitation, and mitigation, with metrics capturing success rates and efficiency in solving complex, multi-stage scenarios.

Key metrics include Unguided % Solved for autonomous performance, Subtask-Guided % Solved for prompted efficacy, and Subtasks % Solved for granular progress. Time to solve the most difficult task highlights efficiency gaps, underscoring the need for robust agentic reasoning in high-stakes domains.
