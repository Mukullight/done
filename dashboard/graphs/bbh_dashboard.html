BIG-Bench Hard (BBH)

BIG-Bench Hard (BBH) is a challenging subset of the BIG-Bench benchmark, curated to focus on tasks where even the strongest language models at the time achieved low performance (< 50%). It includes 23 diverse tasks spanning navigation, logical deduction, multi-step arithmetic, and causal reasoning, designed to probe the limits of model capabilities in complex, human-like reasoning. BBH emphasizes tasks requiring few-shot learning and generalization beyond training data patterns.

The Average score aggregates performance across these tasks, providing a robust measure of frontier model progress. BBH remains a critical evaluation for assessing advancements in reasoning and problem-solving.
